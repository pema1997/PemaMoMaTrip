    Jean-Michel Basquiat. Glenn. 1984

    Jean Michel Basquiat was born in December 22, 1960 in Brooklyn. The painting is called Portrait of Glenn, that the artist made in 1985. Portrait of Glenn is, most probably, a portrait of Glenn O'Brien, who was a writer and journalist in the East Village. He began his career as a Graffiti artist, but he gained his fame through his paintings. He was famous for his visual Vocabulary. Therefore, his painting are popular for not only being a Visual aid but for the way he painted his words in his paintings.
