    A Menace to Liberty 2012

    Andrea Bowers is a Los Angeles-based American artist who is a lifelong advocate for women's righs, immigration, civil rights and climate change. Her works are extremely labor-intensive. This art "A menace to Liberty" is inspired by anarchist Emma Goldman's Journal Mother Earth. The drawing is a collage of random cardboards, carefully selected.
